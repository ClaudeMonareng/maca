About the Founder

Claude Monareng is a passionate, energetic and caring visionary, who lets out his personality speak through his work.

A qualified data scientist by training, an academic facilitator, a Blockchain project manager by experience and a full-stack Web Developer by current profession.

Claude Monareng has always been passionate about helping his community and bringing out the best in others. He is a keen and daring social and commercial entrepreneur who is not afraid to push boundaries against odds.

A proud Joburger, Sowetan and a Witsie, Claude Monareng has spent the most part of his life fighting against the ills of society as an activist in the townships of Soweto.
