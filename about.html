About Us

We are a new, upcoming, innovative and world-class, Mathematics, Science and Digital Technology Academy, in the province of KwaZulu-Natal, Newcastle.

We offer content and lesson delivery in small classes, that is both pioneering and responsive to cater for the special individual needs of each of our learners, so that they get the most from our offering, in order to perform at their optimal level in their mainstream assessment standards.

We are committed to excellence of the highest possible order, to do justice to the ailing mainstream education system, particularly in the fields of science and technology and ensuring that we are well positioned to meaningfully participate and compete with the rest of the world.
